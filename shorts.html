Thai Iced Tea Music Video:

This music video was made for a local band known as "The Slightlys" for my film class in my junior year at Providence High School. The name of this song is "Thai Iced Tea". It's an innocent, fun song about the love of summer-time featuring everyone's loved cold beverage. Small humans were used in the video in order to portray a sense of innocence and simple fun.

*Many cups of Thai Iced Tea were consumed during the making of this video*
