CSUN Gamer Symphony Orchestra

This is a documentary featuring the CSUN Gamer Symphony Orchestra. They are a group of performers at Cal State: Northridge that arrange music from various popular or even niche video games into an orchestral format and hold one concert per semester where they perform various pieces free of charge for the community to enjoy. The documentary highlights the club from the point of view of club president Andrew Krezinger.
